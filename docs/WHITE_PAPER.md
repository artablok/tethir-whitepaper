## Abstract

TETHIR (USD.T) represents a paradigm shift in decentralized digital asset design. Launched in early 2024, this community-driven token has operated for over one year without central authority, banking partnerships, or fiat backing. Unlike traditional stablecoins, TETHIR derives its value purely from community trust and market dynamics.

**Critical Disclaimer:** TETHIR is NOT Tether (USDT). We are NOT a stablecoin. We have NO USD peg and NO fiat backing. Price is determined solely by market forces.

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Vision

TETHIR was created as a social experiment in decentralized value transfer — a digital asset that exists purely through community consensus rather than institutional backing. Our vision challenges the traditional stablecoin model by proving that digital value can emerge organically from network effects and collective trust.

### 1.2 Historical Context

Launched in Q1 2024, the original TETHIR contract (`0x3ede8fb2500862d081b24908e774b25e6d42071a`) has operated continuously for over 365 days, accumulating 3,960+ holders and processing millions of transactions across BNB Chain. This longevity demonstrates the resilience of community-driven assets.

### 1.3 Evolution

Following a comprehensive security audit in Q4 2024, the community voted to migrate to an optimized contract (`0x2728e0daa79c93a97bb5eb86f4e18d47da84730d`). This White Paper documents the historical foundation while the new contract carries forward our community-first principles.
